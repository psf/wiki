[Enfold Systems, Inc.](http://enfoldsystems.com) is a software consultancy *and* product company located mostly in Houston, Texas. Staff members include Python notables [Alan Runyan](./Alan(20)Runyan.html), [Mark Hammond](Mark%20Hammond), and [Sidnei da Silva](./Sidnei(20)da(20)Silva.html). The company is intimately involved in [Plone](../web/Plone) and many follow-on developments.
