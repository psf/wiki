David Beazley is the author of The Python Essential Reference (Addison-Wesley), The Python Cookbook, 3rd Ed (O\'Reilly Media), and creator of several Python-related packages including Swig ([http://www.swig.org](http://www.swig.org)), PLY ([http://www.dabeaz.com/ply](http://www.dabeaz.com/ply)), and Curio ([https://github.com/dabeaz/curio](https://github.com/dabeaz/curio)). A frequent [PyCon](../conferences/pycon/PyCon) conference presenter, he also teaches a variety of computer science and programming courses in Chicago. Further information is available at [http://www.dabeaz.com](http://www.dabeaz.com).
