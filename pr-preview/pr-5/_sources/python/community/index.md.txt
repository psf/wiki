# Community & User Groups

Python's community has always been one of its greatest strengths. This section collects pages about local user groups around the world, advocacy efforts to spread the word about Python, and ways to get involved with the broader community. Much of this content dates from the era when the wiki was the primary coordination hub for community organizing.

## Getting Involved

- [GetInvolved](GetInvolved) — ways to contribute to Python, from documentation to bug fixing to administration
- [PythonWikiMaintainers](PythonWikiMaintainers) — discussion and coordination for wiki contributors
- [PythonDiscourseIdeas](PythonDiscourseIdeas) — hosting documents for discussion on the Python Discourse Ideas category
