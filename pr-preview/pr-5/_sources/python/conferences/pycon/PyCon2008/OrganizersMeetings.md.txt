**Page for PyCon 2009 meetings:** [PyCon2009/Organizers_Meetings](./PyCon2009(2f)Organizers_Meetings.html)

Meetings will be held every other week on Tuesday.

Meetings are held via IRC. We use the \'#pycon\' channel on irc.freenode.net. [Connection details (instructions) here](./PyCon(2f)Organizers_Meetings_Connection_Details.html).

There is a [\"PyCon 2008 Planning\" Google calendar](https://www.google.com/calendar/embed?src=og3ea7a3ov1tb3e18qppkd4ev8%40group.calendar.google.com&mode=MONTH). If you want write access, ask David Goodger or Chris McAvoy (or just ask on [the pycon-organizers list](http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/pycon-organizers)).

[Private PyCon Organizers\' wiki](http://wiki.python.org/moin-pycon/FrontPage) (join pycon-organizers and ask for the user/password info).
