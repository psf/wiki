Dinu Gherman has helped translate many popular Python books to German ([Publications](http://python.net/~gherman/Publications.html)), is the author of several [MacPython](../platforms/MacPython) applications ([Pycotine](http://python.net/~gherman/Pycotine.html), [RegexPlor](http://python.net/~gherman/RegexPlor.html), [ReSTedit](http://python.net/~gherman/ReSTedit.html), [emkey](http://python.net/~gherman/emkey.html) and other [MacOSXStuff](http://python.net/~gherman/MacOSXStuff.html)) and has written many modules for [ReportLab](http://python.net/~gherman/ReportLab.html) .\
