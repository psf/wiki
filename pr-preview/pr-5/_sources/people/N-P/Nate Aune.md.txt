Nate Aune is founder and president of [Jazkarta, Inc.](http://www.jazkarta.com) a Boston-based consulting and solutions provider specializing in Python-based web applications and Plone CMS implementations.

Nate has given talks at [EuroPython](../conferences/europython/EuroPython), [PyCon](../conferences/pycon/PyCon), [PyConBrasil](./PyConBrasil.html) and many Plone conferences. He is also the organizer of the [Boston Django User Group](http://django.meetup.com/6/), [Boston Python User Group](http://python.meetup.com/181/) and the [Boston Plone User Group](http://bostonplone.org).

Nate blogs at [http://nateaune.com](http://nateaune.com) and [http://blog.jazkarta.com](http://blog.jazkarta.com).
