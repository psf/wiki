As you have probably heard, Jython and Django are making the news! The implementation is almost ready and there is a lot of discussion about it\...see the blog segment below.

I\'m very excited that [PyCon](./PyCon.html) 2008 will be underway shortly. Being that I live close to Chicago, I am glad that [PyCon](./PyCon.html) will be in my area this year. It sounds like there is a great lineup for [PyCon](./PyCon.html), and Jython is not going to miss out. Frank Wierzbicki will be hosting the Jython Sprint at the event. Stay tuned for more details!
