# Lists {#Lists}

## Bulleted {#Bulleted}

### Input {#Input}

    My pets:
     * Dog
     * Cat
      * Mouse

### Display {#Display}

My pets:

- Dog
- Cat
  - Mouse

## Numbered {#Numbered}

### Input {#Input-1}

    To do:
     1. Feed dog
     1. Feed cat
      1. Let out mouse

### Display {#Display-1}

To do:

1.  Feed dog
2.  Feed cat
    1.  Let out mouse
