# Lists 

## Bulleted 

### Input 

    My pets:
     * Dog
     * Cat
      * Mouse

### Display 

My pets:

- Dog
- Cat
  - Mouse

## Numbered 

### Input 

    To do:
     1. Feed dog
     1. Feed cat
      1. Let out mouse

### Display 

To do:

1.  Feed dog
2.  Feed cat
    1.  Let out mouse

![(!)](/wiki/modernized/img/idea.png "(!)") MoinMoin can count on its own!

![(!)](/wiki/modernized/img/idea.png "(!)") The number of leading spaces defines the hierarchical level of the list.

There are more examples of lists at [HelpOnLists](HelpOnLists).
