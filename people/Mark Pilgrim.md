Mark Pilgrim is the author of several \"Dive Into\" books, columns, and blogs related to Python, web development (especially accessibility, web services, and RSS feeds), Mac OS X, and himself. He co-authored the [http://feedvalidator.org/](http://feedvalidator.org/) service with [SamRuby](./SamRuby.html), and has written a number of web and web-services-related libraries in Python.\
