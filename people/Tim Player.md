Timplayer is a python and Gstreamer music player for Gnome. It has a GTK interface to integrate well with gnome. I made it because Totem is too large and complex and XMMS has an old interface.

Homepage at [http://creationix.com/OpenSource/TimPlayer/](http://creationix.com/OpenSource/TimPlayer/) Download the newest version at [http://www.creationix.com/packages/timplayer.tar.gz](http://www.creationix.com/packages/timplayer.tar.gz)
