# Mailing Lists and Newsgroups {#Mailing_Lists_and_Newsgroups}

Numerous mailing lists, forums, newsgroups exist covering Python usage in the general sense and in conjunction with specific topics and technologies. Here is a list of places to look for list/group information:

- An official guide to [Python mailing lists and newsgroups](http://www.python.org/community/lists/){.http}.

- A list of [mailing lists hosted at python.org](http://mail.python.org/){.http} - note that some are more active than others.

- The [comp.lang.python newsgroup](CompLangPython).
