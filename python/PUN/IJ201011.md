# PUN/IJ201011

::: {#content dir="ltr" lang="en"}
The next PUN will be held on Thursday October 20th 2011 at the IJ kantine in Amsterdam. The venue is sponsored by [http://minddistrict.nl](http://minddistrict.nl){.http}

We start at 19:30.

Route: [http://www.ijkantine.nl/pagina/13/Route](http://www.ijkantine.nl/pagina/13/Route){.http}

If you plan on joining, put yourself on visitor list down below to give everybody an idea of how many people are coming and who.

**Note: you\'re free to come in any case, registering is just to get a rough idea on the amount of people**.
