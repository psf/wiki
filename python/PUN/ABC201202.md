# PUN/ABC201202

::: {#content dir="ltr" lang="en"}
The next PUN will be held on Wednesday February 15th 2012 at the ABC Treehouse. The venue is sponsored by [ABC Treehouse](http://www.treehouse.abc.nl/){.http}, drinks are sponsored by [Maykin Media](http://www.maykinmedia.nl/){.http}.

**We start at 19:30.**

Route: [http://www.treehouse.abc.nl/index.php?page=location](http://www.treehouse.abc.nl/index.php?page=location){.http}

If you plan on joining, put yourself on visitor list down below to give everybody an idea of how many people are coming and who.

**Note: you\'re free to come in any case, registering is just to get a rough idea on the amount of people**.
