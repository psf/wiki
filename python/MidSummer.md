# MidSummer

::: {#content dir="ltr" lang="en"}
Official mid-summer celebration is on Friday, June 24 this year. If you are in town by then, make sure that you have bought your alcohol at systembolaget beforehand and then go to [SlottsSkogen](./SlottsSkogen.html){.nonexistent} in the evening, if you don\'t have anything better to do (and have no private party). It\'s the most important summer party here.
:::
