# EuroPython 2021 

EuroPython 2021 will take place from July 26 - August 1, online.

See the official site for more details:

- Official website: [https://ep2021.europython.eu/](https://ep2021.europython.eu/)

Follow our blog for updates and more details:

- [EuroPython Blog](https://blog.europython.eu/)

**This page is meant to organize information collected by attendees.**
