# EuroPython 2008 

[EuroPython](EuroPython) 2008 will take place in Vilnius, Lithuania.

The *centre of all things* of [EuroPython](EuroPython) is the [mailing list](http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/europython).

## Meetings 

Details of the IRC meetings can be found here: [/IRCMeetings](./EuroPython(2f)2008(2f)IRCMeetings.html)
