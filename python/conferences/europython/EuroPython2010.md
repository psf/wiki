# EuroPython 2010 

EuroPython 2010 will take place from Monday 19th July to Thursday 22nd July, in Birmingham, UK. There will be two days of tutorials beforehand and two days of sprints afterwards.

See the official site for details:

[http://www.europython.eu/](http://www.europython.eu/)
