# EuroPython 2007 

[EuroPython](EuroPython) 2007 will take place at the [Reval Hotel Lietuva](http://www.revalhotels.com/en/Hotels/Lithuania/Reval-Hotel-Lietuva) in Vilnius, Lithuania from Monday 9th July to Wednesday 11th July.
