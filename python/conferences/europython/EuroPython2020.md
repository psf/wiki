EuroPython 2020 will take place from July 20 - 26, online.

See the official site for more details:

- Official website: [https://ep2020.europython.eu/](https://ep2020.europython.eu/)

Follow our blog for updates and more details:

- [EuroPython Blog](https://blog.europython.eu/)
