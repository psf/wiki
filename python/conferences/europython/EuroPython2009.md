# EuroPython 2009 

EuroPython 2009 will take place from Tuesday 30th June to Thursday 2nd July, in Birmingham, UK. There will be two days of tutorials beforehand and two days of sprints afterwards.

The Talks Schedule has been published at [http://europython.eu/talks/timetable/](http://europython.eu/talks/timetable/)

See the official site for other details:
