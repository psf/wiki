# Invitation - PyCon India 2009 

## http://in.pycon.org 

## What is PyCon India 2009? 

Pycon India 2009 is a community conference by the Indian Python user and developer community.It is aimed at Python programmers, enthusiasts, new comers to Python language as well as users of other languages. The conference is expected to be a mix of Tutorials for beginners and Talks by experts. There would be coding sessions on Open Source Python Projects in the form of Sprints.

## Who\'s organizing? 

The conference will be run by the [PyCon](PyCon) India Society, which is being set up specially for this and future conferences.The Conference and Society have been initiated by [BangPypers](BangPypers) - the Bangalore Python User Group and welcomes Python programmers from other parts of the country to join in. At present python enthusiasts from around the country are working together to organise the conference. There is a mailing list at [inpycon@pycon.org](mailto:inpycon@pycon.org) which is the primary forum for organising this.

## When? 

- 26th and 27th September, 2009

## Where? 

- CSA Lecture Halls, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore

## How to Register? 

- Register at [http://in.pycon.org](http://in.pycon.org) before Aug 1st \[TBD\] (Early Bird Registration : Rs 200/-). Go to the site and register as a user. After you register, you will be encouraged to fill in your profile. Once you fill in your profile, you will be registered as a delegate and the date of registration will be recorded and displayed on your profile to enable you to take advantage of the Early Bird discount. Print out your profile and produce it at the reception desk of the conference. The website has a navigation page with instructions on how to propose a talk or volunteer for helping out.

- Or just drop in at the venue on the event days (Spot Registration : Rs 300/-)
