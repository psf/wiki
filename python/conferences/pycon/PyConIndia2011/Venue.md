# PyCon India 2011 Venue 

## Venue Comparison Criteria 

- Venue Hire Charges
- Lecture Halls
- Local staff and student support
- Accessibility
- Accomodation

## Raja Lakshmi Engineering College 

- Venue Hire Charges
- Lecture Halls
- Local staff and student support
  - Lecturers and Professsors attended last [PyConIndia](./PyConIndia.html). Are willing to support.

  - Some other [Python workshop were conducted before](http://baijum81.livejournal.com/33872.html)
- Accessibility
  - Located away from the city. But it seems REC can make transport arrangements. [http://groups.google.com/group/chennaipy/browse_thread/thread/23f101b66563960c#](http://groups.google.com/group/chennaipy/browse_thread/thread/23f101b66563960c#)
- Accomodation
  - There seems to be lot of hotels nearby, since Sriperumbudur is a tourist spot. [http://groups.google.com/group/chennaipy/browse_thread/thread/23f101b66563960c#](http://groups.google.com/group/chennaipy/browse_thread/thread/23f101b66563960c#)
