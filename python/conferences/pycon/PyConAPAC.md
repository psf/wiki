# PyCon Asia-Pacific 

[PyCon](PyCon) APAC is the regional counterpart of [PyCon](PyCon) in Asia-Pacific. It was inaugurated in Singapore in 2010 and has done or will be doing its rounds at

- Bangladesh
- China
- Hong Kong
- India
- Indonesia
- Japan
- Malaysia
- Philippines
- Singapore
- South Korea
- Taiwan
- Thailand
- Vietnam
