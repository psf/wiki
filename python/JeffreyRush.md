# JeffreyRush

::: {#content dir="ltr" lang="en"}
In 2006, [Jeffrey](http://taupro.com){.http} accepted a full-time position from the [PSF](PSF) as [PythonAdvocacyCoordinator](PythonAdvocacyCoordinator). He was a motivating force in moving [PyCon](PyCon) to Addison (Dallas), Texas in February 2006, acted as site coordinator and is serving as co-chair for [PyCon](PyCon) 2007.

Jeff is also the organizer of the [Dallas Ft. Worth Pythoneers](http://www.dfwpython.org){.http}, the Linux-SIG leader, former president and webmaster for the [DFW Unix Users Group](http://www.dfwuug.org){.http} and organizer of the [Xanadu Project User Group](http://www.sunless-sea.net){.http}.

When not working for the [PSF](PSF) he is self-employed as a consultant and an active developer and promoter of Zope 2, Zope 3, Twisted and the coming AI Singularity. ![;-)](/wiki/europython/img/smile4.png ";-)"){height="16" width="16"}
