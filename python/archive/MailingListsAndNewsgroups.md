# Mailing Lists and Newsgroups 

Numerous mailing lists, forums, newsgroups exist covering Python usage in the general sense and in conjunction with specific topics and technologies. Here is a list of places to look for list/group information:

- An official guide to [Python mailing lists and newsgroups](http://www.python.org/community/lists/).

- A list of [mailing lists hosted at python.org](http://mail.python.org/) - note that some are more active than others.

- The [comp.lang.python newsgroup](CompLangPython).
