- [Python as a First Language](http://www.oreillynet.com/pub/a/network/2000/06/02/magazine/python_first_language.html)

- [Getting Started With Python](http://web.archive.org/web/20010201170400/http://www.sunworld.com/sunworldonline/swol-02-1998/swol-02-python.html)

- [Batteries Included](http://web.archive.org/web/20001013152452/http://sunworld.com/swol-12-1998/swol-12-regex.html)

- [EricRaymond](./EricRaymond.html)\'s [Why Python](http://www.linuxjournal.com/article.php?sid=3882)

- [Python Streamlines Space Shuttle Mission Design](https://staging.python.org/success-stories/usa/)

- [Introduction of Machine Learning Library Scikit-Learn](https://www.jmlr.org/papers/volume12/pedregosa11a/pedregosa11a.pdf): the paper appeared in Journal of Machine Learning Research 2011
