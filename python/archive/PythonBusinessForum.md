The PythonBusinessForum is an international society registered in Sweden on May 1st, 2002. Its purpose is to organise and further the interests of companies that base their business on the [OpenSource](OpenSource) programming language [Python](Python).

Formerly at www.python-in-business.org which has been taken over by a spam site.
