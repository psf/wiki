## Sprint Locations {#Sprint_Locations}

The sprints will take place in Building 40 in the rooms in the four \"corners\" (like for the parallel sessions), on the **ground floor** (not downstairs!), just a few steps (\"half a stair\") \"above\" the Cafe-area. There will be signs on the doors as well.

## Pre-Conference Sprints {#Pre-Conference_Sprints}

Whoever wants to participate in a pre-conference sprint, please send a mail to [Euro-Loc.Secretariat@cern.ch](mailto:Euro-Loc.Secretariat@cern.ch){.mailto} telling us so, as we need to arrange for access to the CERN site for the weekend (before registration). \-- [AndreasPfeiffer](./AndreasPfeiffer.html){.nonexistent} (for the local organisers)
