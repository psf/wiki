# Birds of a Feather Sessions

Projects and groups are encouraged to hold meetings, dinners and other activities at PyCon.

**Be sure to use the Open Space Scheduling Board to confirm room availability.**

Also see: [Open Space](./Open(20)Space.html){.reference .external}

The following birds-of-a-feather sessions are being planned:

- [Zope Foundation](http://foundation.zope.org){.http .reference .external} members BoF
- [BeerBoF](./BeerBoF.html){.nonexistent .reference .external}: Noble Python programmers will set off in search of the Chicago\'s rare \"local micros\". Upon encountering the **beers**, the coders will consume them with great vigor.
- [ClimbBoF](ClimbBoF){.reference .external}: Let\'s find a local climbing gym to feed our addiction and talk about Python a bit. Maybe we can meet up with the BeerBof afterwards! :)
- [Trac](http://trac.edgewall.org){.http .reference .external} BoF: Meet in front of La Guardia at 5pm in search of dinner, back for Q&A and hacking in La Guardia at 7pm.
- [Chicago Python User Group](http://chipy.org){.http .reference .external} Meeting: Thursday night 7 pm. Swing by after you check in!
- [EduPython2008](EduPython2008){.reference .external} : The annual BoF for discussing Python in Education\... or Education in Python\... or\...
- [EnsoBoF](./EnsoBoF.html){.nonexistent .reference .external} : \[[http://www.humanized.com](http://www.humanized.com){.http .reference .external} Enso\] (a Python-based command interface) is now open-source! Come learn how to develop for it and talk about its future direction. Friday AM, wherever is free \-- check the physical bulletin board.
- [PyConRecordingBof](PyConRecordingBof){.reference .external}: the conference will leave us with a large pile of audio and video data to be edited and published. We need to form a group of volunteer editors to process all this material.
- How to start and maintain a local Python Users Group
- BuildBoF: Buildbot users and developers gather together
- [TexasPythoneers](TexasPythoneers){.reference .external} Start planning the next Texas Regional Un-Conference in Austin.
- [Python and Scrum](./Python(20)and(20)Scrum.html){.nonexistent .reference .external} Roundtable discussion regarding the impact of Python in a Scrum shop, and the contrary. Friday lunch?
- [PackagingBOF](PackagingBOF){.reference .external} : Discussion about packaging and distribution of Python libraries (eggs and distutils). Friday or Saturday.
- [ScienceBOF](./ScienceBOF.html){.nonexistent .reference .external} : Discussion of Python in Science including project ideas.
- [web2py meeting](http://mdp.cti.depaul.edu/examples/static/web2py_comics.pdf){.http .reference .external} in room Love Int\'l A at 2:10pm on Saturday.
- [NetflixPrizeBOF](NetflixPrizeBOF){.reference .external} : Discuss approaches to the Netflix prize, getting started with PyFlix, algorithm performance, etc
