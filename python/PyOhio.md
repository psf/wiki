### PyOhio 2009

**PyOhio**

**July 25-26, 2009**

**Columbus, OH**

PyOhio 2009 will be held at [Knowlton Hall at Ohio State University](http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&source=s_q&hl=en&geocode=&q=275+W+Woodruff+Ave,+Columbus,+OH&sll=40.013515,-83.014455&sspn=0.022811,0.04755&ie=UTF8&ll=40.002372,-83.012695&spn=5.839762,12.172852&z=7&iwloc=A){.http .reference .external} on [July 25-26, 2009](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&amp;tmeid=YTkwbjJlcjRjNGFsNzhvMmhxN2VxajVxZW8gY2F0aGVyaW5lLmRldmxpbkBt&amp;tmsrc=Y2F0aGVyaW5lLmRldmxpbkBnbWFpbC5jb20){.http .reference .external}.

**Sponsorship opportunities are now available** - see our [Sponsorship Prospectus](./PyOhio(2f)Prospectus.html){.reference .external}.

PyOhio 2008 was great! Thanks to all volunteers, speakers, attendees, and sponsors! See [last year\'s wiki page](PyOhio08){.reference .external} for details.

Main [PyOhio](http://pyohio.org){.http .reference .external} page is now online.

> 

You can join a [GroupChat](http://www.meebo.com/room/pyohio){.http .reference .external} during the event.

> 

PyOhio is a daylong regional miniconference. A one-day PyOhio was held July 26, 2008; for 2009, we are extending PyOhio to a full weekend.

> 

Watch this page for news on PyOhio! To receive PyOhio announcements, please join the PyOhio mailing list: [http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/pyohio](http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/pyohio){.http .reference .external}

To help make PyOhio happen, please join the organizers\' mailing list: [http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/pyohio-organizers](http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/pyohio-organizers){.http .reference .external}
