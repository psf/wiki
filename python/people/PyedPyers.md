[PyedPypers](http://www.pyedpypers.org) is a community where you can upload your project, comment on other Python game projects, build teams, ask for technical help, and discuss everything about game development with Python.

Formed early in 2009, as of February 2010 Pyed numbers about 50 members.

Pyed maintains an IRC channel, #pyedpypers, on irc.freenode.net.
