Roundup is a simple-to-use/install issue-tracking system with command-line, web and e-mail interfaces. It is based on the winning design from [KaPingYee](KaPingYee) in the [SoftwareCarpentry](./SoftwareCarpentry.html) \"Track\" design competition.

It\'s a [SourceForge](SourceForge) project, with the lead developer being [RichardJones](RichardJones). You can visit the project page at [http://roundup.sf.net/](http://roundup.sf.net/) for more information.

## Roundup for python.org
