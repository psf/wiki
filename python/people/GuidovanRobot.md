Guido van Robot ([http://gvr.sf.net](http://gvr.sf.net)) is a teaching language designed to introduce new programmers to fundamental programming concepts. This sprint is being organized primarily to introduce the next generation of gvr hackers to the code and have them leave familiar enough with it to maintain and add to it in the future.

The current list of participants includes:

- Paul Carduner - coach and project leader
- Steve Howell
- Owen Burridge
- Eldar Omuraliev
- Owen Kern
- James Frick-Tordella
- Marjorie Guillen

The GvR sprint will take place on Monday, March 22nd and Tuesday, March 23rd from 10 am until 2 pm.
