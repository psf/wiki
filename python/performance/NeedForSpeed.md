The NeedForSpeed sprint took place from May 21 to May 28 2006 in Reykjavik, Iceland. The principal sponsor is [EWT, LLC](http://www.ewtcareers.com/) with local support from [CCP Games](http://www.ccpgames.com/).

The following pages were used to plan and report on the sprint:

- [/Goals](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Goals.html): An area to discuss each of the sprint goals, and to allow everyone to start planning what they will be working on

- [/Tasks](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Tasks.html): What people are currently working on. Also see

  - [ListOfPerformanceRelatedPatches](ListOfPerformanceRelatedPatches)

- [/Toolsets](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Toolsets.html): What should I install before I come?

- [/Participants](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Participants.html): Who came, and information they have added about themselves

- [/Sponsors](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Sponsors.html): The organizations behind the sprint

- [/Venue](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Venue.html): Information about the Grand Hotel and Reykjavik along with some background information about Iceland

- [/Tourism](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Tourism.html): You\'re in Iceland \-- what do you do?

- [/Travel Itineraries](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Travel(20)Itineraries.html): At long last\.....

- [/Successes](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Successes.html): Changes that appear to have achieved a measurable speedup

- [/Failures](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Failures.html): Changes that have been tried without producing any speed gain and are therefore probably dead ends

- [/Deferred](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Deferred.html): Things that might help, but were not looked at during the sprint

- [/Schedule](./NeedForSpeed(2f)Schedule.html): Fixed events in the week we\'d like everyone to be part of

- [/FollowUp](./NeedForSpeed(2f)FollowUp.html): What\'s left to do

- [/IRCLog](./NeedForSpeed(2f)IRCLog.html): Conversation that happened on IRC during the sprint.
