# EuroPython 2007 - final week of online registration! {#EuroPython_2007_-_final_week_of_online_registration.21}

The final week of [EuroPython](EuroPython) online registration is here, with registration via the conference Web site ending on Monday 2nd July. [EuroPython](EuroPython) 2007 will take place in Vilnius, Lithuania on Monday 9th, Tuesday 10th and Wednesday 11th July.

More information on registration can be found here:

- [How to register](http://www.europython.org/sections/registration_issues/how-to-register){.http}

It\'s not too late to plan your trip to Vilnius and to see influential figures from the Python community (and other communities) in action:

- [Keynotes](http://www.europython.org/sections/events/keynotes/){.http}, featuring Guido van Rossum (creator of Python), Simon Willison (co-creator of Django) and David Axmark (co-founder of MySQL)

- [Invited speakers](http://www.europython.org/sections/events/invited-speakers/){.http}, including Arlo Belshee (agile development expert)

- A comprehensive [programme](http://www.europython.org/timetable){.http} of talks, panels, [sprints](http://www.europython.org/sections/sprints_and_wiki/){.http}, [Open Space](http://www.europython.org/sections/events/open_space){.http} and [Lightning Talks](http://www.europython.org/sections/events/lightning_talks){.http}

See you in Vilnius!
