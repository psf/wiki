# EuroPython 2008 {#EuroPython_2008}

[EuroPython](EuroPython) 2008 will take place in Vilnius, Lithuania.

The *centre of all things* of [EuroPython](EuroPython) is the [mailing list](http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/europython){.http}.
