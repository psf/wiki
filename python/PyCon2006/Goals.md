# PyCon2006/Goals

::: {#content dir="ltr" lang="en"}
The targets for [PyCon](PyCon) 2006 are:

- 3 days of conference programming
- 4 days of sprints (all after the conference)
- 3 paper tracks
- 450-500 attendees

These goals are subject to revision depending on how [PyCon](PyCon) 2005 does.
